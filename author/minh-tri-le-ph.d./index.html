I am a recent Ph.D. graduate in deep learning seeking an R&D position in AI based in Grenoble area or remotely. I am passionnate about AI, innovation, R&D, and software development.

In July 2023, I defended my Ph.D. on deep learning for industrial sensor-based applications on the most constrained devices in the state-of-the-art #TinyML with TDK InvenSense and Inria Grenoble Alpes in the Statify research team.

My interests include deep learning, model compression, pruning, knowledge distilliation, quantization, tinyML, edge inference.
Learn more about my Ph.D. subject here, or check out this summary of my thesis.
